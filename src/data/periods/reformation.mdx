Martin Luther, a German monk and professor, recovered the biblical doctrine of justification by faith alone.

### Sola Scriptura
His Ninety-Five Theses, posted on October 31, 1517, challenged the corruption of indulgences and ignited the Reformation. The principles of **sola scriptura**, **sola fide**, and **sola gratia** returned the church to apostolic Christianity.
